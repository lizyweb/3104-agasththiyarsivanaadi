Know About Astrology

The information is based on palm leaf manuscripts written by 18 (Rishi Muni) sages who lived 5000 years ago.

Nadi Astrology is in the form of Hindu astrology proficient in Tamil Nadu, Kerala and beside inner citys in India.The prominent theory of Nadi Astrology is “Nadi”.

There are 150 Nadis in a sign or Rashi, one sign is 30 degrees of zodiac. Twelve signs of zodiac arrange into three divisions. Movable (Chara), Fixed (Sthira) and Dual (Dvisvabhava) signs Nomenclature of 150 Nadis is peculiar to each of these three types of signs.
